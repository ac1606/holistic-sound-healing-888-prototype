Why I Do This

Music has always carried me through

Music has carried me through my own transformation, and I've spent years sharing it with children and adults alike. Sound healing isn't separate from who I am. It's how I show up, every day, for the people I work with.

I've spent years teaching piano and singing, and just as long learning to hold space for people going through their own healing. Every session blends the two: the discipline of music with the gentleness of sound healing.

Heal one, heal all

That phrase is the whole idea behind this practice. I help the person in front of me, and when that helps you show up better for the people around you, it keeps going from there.
